About me!

I grew up in Newfoundland, enjoying outdoor actvities, and have always wanted it to be my permanent home. After graduating from Memorial University with a degree in Visual Arts, I traveled to Western Canada where I worked for a few years. In 2019 I made the move back to Newfoundland to pursue a post diploma in Geographic Information Systems (GIS). It tied together my visual arts skills with my love for the outdoors. In my GIS diploma we did some sections in HTML and CSS and SQL which I really enjoyed. When I heard about Get Coding I thought it sounded like an amazing opportunity and tied in nicely with my skills. I am loving learning from professionals in the tech world and am excited about what my future holds!
